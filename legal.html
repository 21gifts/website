Privacy Policy

Overview

21.gifts ("we", "us") operates the website at 21.gifts. We do not run advertising, sell data, or track visitors. This page explains what data is involved.

Data on this website

This page is a static site. It does not set cookies, does not load analytics scripts, and does not collect personal data. Your browser only loads HTML, CSS, and a tiny inline script for the mobile menu.

Hosting

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may record technical data such as IP addresses in standard server logs as part of its infrastructure. For details, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy .

Data in the app

The 21.gifts application (a separate domain) processes data needed to publish NOSTR events and resolve Lightning Addresses. By design, the server never sees your private NOSTR key, and it never holds funds. NOSTR events you publish are public on the relay. Lightning payments go directly from the donor's wallet to the receiver's Lightning Address.

Contact

If you contact us by email, we will process the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, and we will not pass it on to third parties.

Your rights

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Write to info@21.gifts .
